Missing that special someone across the miles?
Two Halves bridge the gap, letting you stay connected in a whole new way.

The Story


Our long-distance journey started with a sweet hack: using home cameras for those quick, reassuring glimpses of each other. While it sparked connection, video calls felt disruptive and existing smart home features lacked the emotional touch. We craved a tool specifically designed for long-distance love.

Two Halves was born from this need. We envisioned a way to express emotions, share moments, and feel closer, even across miles.
